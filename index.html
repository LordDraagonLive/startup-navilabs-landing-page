FAQ

Do you sell a product?

No. We provide services—dedicated teams and custom solutions. If a product emerges from an engagement, it’s bespoke to that client.

What’s your engagement model?

Flexible: team augmentation, fixed‑scope builds, or long‑term retainer for roadmap ownership and support.

Where do you operate?

Headquartered in Sri Lanka with clients across Asia and Europe. Remote‑first, security‑minded delivery.

Do you provide post-launch support?

Yes. We offer ongoing maintenance, monitoring, incident response, and roadmap delivery. Engagements can be SLA-backed retainers or time-and-materials, depending on your needs.

How quickly can we start?

Discovery can start in 1–2 weeks. Team augmentation typically starts within 2–4+ weeks based on skill fit and availability. If you need a dedicated team or specific senior profiles we don’t have free immediately, we’ll align timelines (often 4–8 weeks, including any hiring/ramp-up) and keep you updated transparently.

Can you join our in-house team remotely?

Absolutely. We embed into your workflows (Slack/Teams, standups, shared boards), align to your sprint cadence, and ensure strong timezone overlap across UK/EU/Asia.
